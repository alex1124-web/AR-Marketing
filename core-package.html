Who this is for

Core bundles your Meta and Instagram advertising, the website those ads point to, and the automation that follows up after someone clicks — all managed by one team instead of three vendors who've never spoken to each other. It's built for Southern California businesses (Los Angeles, Orange County, Riverside, and beyond) that are ready to actually invest in growth, not just run some ads and hope. If you're past the "let's just try something" stage and want a system that's actually built to work together, this is the package.

Meta & Instagram Ads

The ad management piece means Facebook and Instagram campaigns built around your actual buyers — not broad reach for its own sake. That includes audience research, creative testing, and ongoing optimization, not a campaign that gets set up once and left alone. You get real reporting on what's working, not a black box you have to take on faith. Blackline Systems, a Los Angeles marketing software company, grew to $9K/month in revenue running Meta ads through this exact process — see the case on the homepage.

Your website, included

A lot of ad budget gets wasted because the campaign is good but the landing page and follow-up aren't. That's exactly why the website isn't an upsell here — it's included. The site your ads point to is engineered to convert, not just look good, and it's built by the same team running the ads, so there's no gap between what the campaign promises and what the page actually delivers.

Automation, included

Also included: the automation layer behind the site — lead follow-up, booking, and reporting handled automatically instead of manually. Most leads go cold because nobody responded fast enough, not because the offer was bad. Automation exists so a lead gets a response within minutes of reaching out, even if nobody's physically at a desk when it happens.

How it actually works

Every Core engagement runs the same four-step process. Audit — we tear apart your existing accounts, creative, and funnel to find exactly where budget is leaking, or if you're starting fresh, where the obvious opportunities are. Strategy — a media plan built around your actual unit economics, not generic best practices copied from a blog post. Launch — campaigns go live with a testing framework built in from day one, not bolted on after the fact. Scale — winners get more budget, underperforming angles get killed, and the account compounds instead of stalling out after the first month.

The AI Agent add-on

The AI Agent — which handles conversations with leads automatically, not just follow-up reminders — is available as an add-on to Core, or included outright if you go with the Full Package instead. Most clients start with Core and decide whether they want that layer once they see how much volume the ads are actually generating.

Questions About Core

How much ad spend do I need to get started?
There's no universal minimum — it depends on your market and offer, which is exactly what the audit step figures out. What matters more than the starting number is having enough runway to get through the 1-2 week calibration period before judging results.

I already have a website. Do I have to replace it?
No — if your existing site converts well, we can point ad traffic there instead of rebuilding it from scratch. Most Core clients end up rebuilding anyway once they see how much a conversion-focused site changes performance, but it's not a requirement.

Can I add the AI Agent later?
Yes. The AI Agent is available as an add-on to Core anytime, or included outright if you upgrade to the Full Package.

Pricing

Ad spend and current setup drive the number here, so a flat price wouldn't be honest. That's why Core starts with an audit call — we look at your situation first, then tell you what it actually costs.
