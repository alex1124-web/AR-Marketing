Who this is for

Full includes everything in Core — the ads, the website, the automation — plus the AI Agent built in from day one instead of bolted on later. This is the package for businesses where speed of response is the actual bottleneck, not lead volume. If you're already comfortable with how Core works and the leads coming in are starting to outpace how fast your team can personally respond to each one, Full is the natural next step — not a totally different product, just Core with the response-speed problem solved at the source.

Everything from Core, still included

Meta and Instagram ad campaigns built around your actual buyers. The website those ads point to, engineered to convert. The automation layer handling lead follow-up, booking, and reporting. None of that changes moving from Core to Full — you're not trading anything away, you're adding one specific capability on top of the same foundation.

What the AI Agent actually does

The AI Agent handles the conversation after a lead comes in — answering common questions, qualifying interest, and getting a real prospect booked or handed off to you, often within the same minute they reached out. Most businesses don't lose leads because the ads didn't work; they lose them because nobody responded fast enough. By the time a human gets around to it, the lead has already called someone else. The AI Agent exists specifically to close that gap, working the same hours your ads are running — including nights and weekends, when most businesses have nobody watching.

Why this instead of hiring someone

The alternative to the AI Agent is usually a person whose whole job is answering the phone or the DMs fast enough — which works, but costs a full-time salary and still has gaps nights, weekends, and whenever they're unavailable. The AI Agent doesn't replace a real team member for everything, but for the specific job of "respond immediately and qualify the lead," it doesn't take breaks.

Setup and how it's trained

The AI Agent is set up around your actual business — the offers you run, the questions your leads actually ask, and the tone you want representing you. It's not a generic chatbot dropped onto your site; it's configured specifically for what you sell and how you sell it, as part of the onboarding process when you come on as a Full Package client.

Pricing

Like every package, pricing depends on your setup — ad spend, current systems, and what the AI Agent actually needs to know to do its job well. Every project starts with a call, not a price list.
