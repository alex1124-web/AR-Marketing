FAQ

Questions you're probably already asking.

How fast will I see results?

Ad platforms need time to learn — expect the first 1–2 weeks to be a calibration period, with real, comparable data by month two. Anyone promising instant results on Meta ads isn't being straight with you.

Is there a contract, or can I cancel anytime?

Month-to-month. No long-term lock-in — if it's not working for you, you can walk away anytime.

Do I keep the ad account and website if I stop working with you?

Everything — the ad account, the website, the automations — runs on our system, so you get one dashboard and one point of contact instead of managing scattered logins yourself. If you leave, that access ends with the engagement, same as most managed marketing services.

What's actually included in the price?

Every Core package includes Meta & Instagram ads, your website, and the automation behind it. The AI Agent is an optional add-on, or included outright in the Full Package. Just need a site with no ads? There's a Website-only package too. Exact pricing depends on your setup, which is what the call is for.

Do you guarantee a certain number of leads?

No — and be skeptical of anyone who does. We'll give you honest, industry-benchmark expectations going in, and report real numbers once you're live, but no ad platform can guarantee outcomes it doesn't fully control.

What if my campaign isn't performing?

That's what the Audit → Strategy → Launch → Scale process is for — underperforming angles get killed, winners get more budget, and creative gets refreshed regularly instead of letting a slow campaign just sit.
