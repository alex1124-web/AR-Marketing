Who this is for

The Website Package is a site designed and built to convert, with no ad management bundled in and no pressure to add it later — for Southern California businesses (Los Angeles, Orange County, Riverside, and beyond) that aren't ready for paid ads, or don't want them. Maybe referrals already cover you, maybe you're testing an idea before committing spend to it, or maybe you just need a website that actually represents the business you've built.

What you actually get

You get a fully custom website — not a templated theme with your logo swapped in. Every site is designed around what your specific business sells and who it sells to, built mobile-first since that's where most of your visitors will actually land, and structured so the path from "landed on the page" to "contacted you" is as short as possible. Typical builds go live within two weeks of kickoff, depending on how much content and feedback turnaround is on your end. Scrubbing SoCal, a Riverside house and pressure washing business, is a live example of exactly this kind of build — see it on the homepage, or call Carlos directly and ask him how the process went.

That includes real copywriting, not filler text — pages built around what a visitor actually needs to see before they'll trust you enough to reach out. It includes mobile optimization as a first-class concern, not an afterthought, since most visitors are on their phones. And it includes basic technical SEO from day one (proper page structure, fast load times, the fundamentals search engines actually check) so the site isn't starting from a hole the moment it goes live.

Add-ons, if you want them later

Automation & Workflows and the AI Agent are both available as add-ons whenever you're ready — starting with just a website doesn't lock you out of the rest of the system. Automation covers things like instant lead follow-up, booking flows, and reporting, so leads don't sit unanswered just because nobody was at a desk. The AI Agent goes a step further and can actually hold a conversation with a visitor or lead on your behalf, qualifying interest before it ever reaches you.

What it doesn't include

This package doesn't include Meta or Instagram ad management — if you want traffic actively driven to the site, that's the Core or Full Package. Nothing about starting here commits you to adding ads later; plenty of clients stay on just the Website Package indefinitely, and that's a completely normal way to work with us.

Questions About the Website Package

Typical builds go live within two weeks of kickoff, depending on how much content and feedback turnaround is on your end.

Yes. Automation & Workflows and the AI Agent are both available as add-ons whenever you're ready — nothing has to be rebuilt to add them.

Pricing

How many pages, what content you already have, and what needs to be built from scratch all shape the number here — there's no fixed price to quote honestly, which is why every project starts with a call instead of a price list.
